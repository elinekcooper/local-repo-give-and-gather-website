HOW IT WORKS

from farms...

In the fields and gardens, farmers grow fresh veggies, but not all make it to stores due to imperfections. "Give and Gather" steps in, partnering with local farmers to rescue these surplus vegetables. We're all about embracing imperfection and supporting sustainable practices.




..to us...

At our hub, we sort through these rescued veggies, picking out the best for our veg boxes. Packed with care by our team and our volunteers, each box is a celebration of freshness and a commitment to reducing waste. It's our way of bringing a taste of sustainability straight to your doorstep.




...to you

Unpack your "Give and Gather" veg box and you're not just getting veggies – you're helping local and global communities. Each bite is a reminder of our efforts to nourish both people and the planet. Together, we're making a delicious impact on sustainability, one rescued vegetable at a time.
