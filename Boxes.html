Boxes


Experience the convenience and goodness of Give and Gather's veg boxes available for pick-up in-store. Handpicked with care, our veg boxes boast an assortment of seasonal vegetables sourced directly from local farms, ensuring freshness and flavor in every bite. Available in a range of sizes to suit your needs, from solo dinners to family feasts, each box offers a delightful variety of content, showcasing the vibrant colors and flavors of the season. Whether you're a seasoned chef seeking culinary inspiration or a busy individual looking for healthy meal solutions, our veg boxes are the perfect choice. Visit our store today and discover the joy of cooking with fresh, locally sourced produce from Give and Gather.

our boxes:

each box comes in sizes of: small (500g), medium (1 kg) and large (3 kg)

Our veg boxes are crafted from eco-friendly wood, designed to be sturdy and reusable. When you return your empty box to our store, we'll gladly sanitize it and put it back into circulation, reducing waste and minimizing our environmental footprint. Did you know that by reusing each box just once, we save enough energy to power a 60-watt light bulb for 105 hours? It's our small yet impactful way of contributing to a greener planet.
